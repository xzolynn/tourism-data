## THREE CONCRETE NUDGE INTERVENTIONS

### #1: EMOTIONAL MESSAGING (Fastest ROI)
- **Finding:** Emotional content = 0.88/1.0 intensity; practical = 0.63/1.0
- **Intervention:** Change "Visit Tojinbo temple" → "Discover healing that changed Riku's heart"
- **Method:** A/B test emotional vs practical on WeChat (2 weeks, 5k per cohort)
- **Expected Result:** +25-35% higher click-through rate
- **Timeline:** Implementation in Week 3-4

### #2: PACKAGED ITINERARIES BY ARCHETYPE (Highest Conversion)
- **Finding:** Three visitor types with different spending profiles identified
- **Intervention:** Create 3 packages (Luxury ¥400k, Experience ¥250k, Budget ¥150k)
- **Method:** Pre-book transportation, accommodation, activities
- **Expected Result:** +45% booking conversion (vs custom itinerary)
- **Timeline:** Implementation in Month 1-2

### #3: RIKU EVENT CAMPAIGNS (Proven 27% Boost)
- **Finding:** Statistical evidence that Riku events increase visitors by 27% (p < 0.004)
- **Intervention:** Time limited offers ("Only 48 hours before Riku's birthday")
- **Method:** Deploy "Scarcity + Loss aversion" nudges 2 weeks before event
- **Expected Result:** Baseline 1,413 visitors/mo → 1,793 visitors/mo during events
- **Timeline:** Ongoing, aligned with Riku event calendar
