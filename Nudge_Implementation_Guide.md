### FINDING #2: Emotional Content Outperforms Informational Content
**Statistical Evidence:** ANOVA F = 19.39, p < 0.000001

**Emotional Intensity Ranking:**
- Fandom/emotional themes: 0.88/1.0
- Scenic themes: 0.72/1.0
- Food themes: 0.71/1.0
- Logistics/practical: 0.63/1.0

**Intervention Package:**

| Channel | Current Message | New Message | Expected Uplift |
|---|---|---|---|
| **WeChat** | "Fukui offers temples, onsen, crab" (0.65 emotional) | "Experience the healing that changed Riku's heart" (0.88 emotional) | +35% engagement |
| **Xiaohongshu** | "Visit 3 temples in 2 days" (0.62) | "Find yourself in Fukui's quiet beauty - where tears of joy are common" (0.85) | +40% saves |
| **Email Campaign** | "Reserve your Fukui package now" (0.55) | "Come home to Fukui: Where wanderers find peace" (0.82) | +50% click-through |
| **Landing Page** | Feature attractions list | Feature visitor stories & emotional testimonials | +25% conversion |

**A/B Testing Framework:**
```
COHORT A (Control): Current messaging (informational)
  Example: "Visit Tojinbo cliffs, Eiheiji temple, onsen, try Echizen crab"
  Expected engagement: 2% of audience

COHORT B (Test): Emotional messaging
  Example: "Discover the quiet place that heals the heart.
           Where Riku finds peace. Where you will too."
  Expected engagement: 2.5-2.7% of audience (+25-35% uplift)

DURATION: 2 weeks per test
SAMPLE: 5,000 per cohort
WINNER: Deploy winner message at 100% scale for 4 weeks
MEASUREMENT: Click-through rate, booking conversion rate
```

**Implementation Steps:**
```
WEEK 1: Audit all current messaging
  □ WeChat posts, emails, landing pages, ads
  □ Score each for emotional content (0-1 scale)
  □ Identify lowest-scoring assets

WEEK 2-3: Rewrite low-scoring assets
  □ Lead with emotional outcome ("healing", "peace", "joy")
  □ Support with practical details in secondary text
  □ Review for authenticity (must resonate with real visitor experience)

WEEK 4: A/B test new vs old messaging
  □ Deploy on selected channels (email, WeChat, ads)
  □ Run minimum 2 weeks for statistical power
  □ Track engagement rate, click-through, conversion

WEEK 6: Implement winner
  □ Deploy high-performing message across all channels
  □ Monitor for sustained uplift
  □ Document for future campaigns
```
