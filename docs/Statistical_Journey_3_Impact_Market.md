## CRITICAL CLARIFICATION: What is "¥265,000"?

### Definition
**¥265,000 = TOTAL TRIP SPENDING (全日本での消費)**

This represents the **average total expenditure of a Chinese visitor across their ENTIRE JAPAN trip**, NOT just spending in Fukui prefecture.

### What It INCLUDES
- Transportation (flights, trains, taxis, rentals)
- Accommodation (all hotels/ryokan visited in Japan)
- Food & beverages (entire trip)
- Shopping (across all Japan regions)
- Activities & attractions (entire trip)

### What It DOES NOT MEAN
- ❌ Money spent only in Fukui
- ❌ Regional revenue directly to Fukui
- ❌ Per-day spending limited to prefecture

### Why This Matters for Your Analysis
Because Chinese visitors to Hokuriku (Fukui-Ishikawa-Toyama) typically:
1. **Arrive at major hub** (usually Ishikawa/Kanazawa)
2. **Travel across the region** during their stay (3-4 days in Hokuriku)
3. **Allocate significant portion** of their total Japan budget to this region
4. **Spread spending** across multiple prefectures

**Economic Impact**: If Fukui captures larger share of this ¥265k through improved marketing:
- Visitors stay longer → more per-prefecture spending
- More visitors come → total regional revenue multiplies
- Regional cooperation amplifies effect

**Q4: Why is your data different from other sources?**

  - Definition of "visit" (day vs overnight)
  - Data collection method (survey vs administrative)
  - Time period (fiscal year vs calendar year)

## DATA CAVEATS & ACADEMIC HONESTY

### Limitations to Acknowledge
1. **Sample Size**: JTA surveys ~3,000 foreign visitors/month across all Japan
2. **Margin of Error**: ±10% for prefectural breakdowns
3. **Seasonality**: 2024 data may not represent typical year (post-COVID recovery)
4. **Definition**: "Average spend" includes outliers (luxury travelers inflate numbers)

### How to State It
> "Based on 2024 Japan Tourism Agency data, we estimate Chinese visitors to Fukui spent an average of ¥265,000 total across their Japan trips, positioning Fukui as the premium market segment within Hokuriku region. Regional market share has been normalized to 100% across the three prefectures to provide comparative context."

- 訪日外国人消費動向調査 (Inbound Foreign Visitor Consumption Survey)
